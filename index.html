Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kinder (3-6 years) is a crucial stage for cognitive, social, and emotional growth. Children develop foundational skills in language, motor coordination, and problem-solving.

Elementary School

Elementary school provides foundational education in subjects like reading, math, and science. It helps children develop essential social, cognitive, and emotional skills for future learning.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school students, ages 10 to 16, transition from childhood to adolescence, developing greater independence and critical thinking skills. They experience increased academic challenges while navigating social and emotional growth.
